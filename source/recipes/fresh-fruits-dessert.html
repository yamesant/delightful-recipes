First Recipe

  • Preparation steps

    1. Wash all fruits.
    2. Cut each apple into eighths. Cut out the seeds.
    3. Cut each pear into quarters. Cut out the seeds.
    4. Peel the banana. Cut it into rings.
    5. Serve on a plate with a fork

  • Ingredients

    • Two apples
    • One pear
    • One banana

  • Equipment

    • Kitchen sink with running water
    • Knife
    • Cutting board
    • Plate
    • Fork
