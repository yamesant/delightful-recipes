Tuna Sandwich

  • Preparation steps

    1. Toast the bread slice.
    2. Use a spoon to mix tuna with mayonnaise in a bowl.
    3. Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
    4. Spread the mixture onto the bread slice.

  • Ingredients

    • Canned Tuna
    • Mayonnaise
    • Slice of Bread
    • Salt and Pepper

  • Equipment

    • Toaster
    • Bowl
    • Spoon
